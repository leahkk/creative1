About Me

Hi! My name is Leah, and I've been cooking since I was at least ten years-old. My parents didn't buy many sweets, so once I learned that I could make chocolate chip cookies, I would make them weekly. I did that for many years, and eventually decided that I wanted to get into baking in general.

Cupcakes were really popular at one time during my teenage years, so I tried those out for a while. After moving out of my parent's house, I was forced to step out of my comfort zone and try out more "useful" cooking, like making dinner and breakfast, etc. I found out that I loved it! Not only could I make whatever dessert I wanted, I realized that I also could make delicious meals that I would eat everyday! (Because you gotta eat everyday.)

Most of the things I've gotten into, such as making wedding cakes, have happened out of a rash decision to try something for a friend. And it has worked out so far! I think people are holding themselves back when it comes to cooking. You don't have to go out to eat if you want something fancy, the fanciest (and most inexpensive) meal can be made by you! I hope to educate the whole world one day about the health benefits, and life benefits in general that come from making your own food.
